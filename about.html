About Verrazano Plumbing

Family-owned, full-service plumbing for Brooklyn and NYC — backed by more than 18 years of experience and a licensed master plumber on every job.

Who We Are

A family business built on doing it right.

Verrazano Plumbing Contractors Inc. is a full-service, family-owned and operated company providing professional service to both the commercial and residential sectors — with the ability to service and install complete plumbing systems.

In addition to preventive-maintenance checks, service of existing equipment, and installation of new equipment, we can help you find ways to reduce your energy bills with high-efficiency equipment. Backed by more than 18 years of experience, our team of dedicated plumbers is ready to meet your plumbing needs. We consider all jobs, small or large.

What sets us apart

  • A licensed NYC Master Plumber (#2479) stands behind every job.
  • We serve homes, apartment buildings, and commercial properties alike.
  • Honest, upfront estimates — and free estimates on new work.
  • 24/7 availability for true plumbing emergencies.

From your first call to the finished job, we treat your property like our own — clean, courteous, and on schedule.
