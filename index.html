NYC Compliance

Local Law 152 gas piping inspections — done right.

NYC Local Law 152 requires periodic inspection of building gas piping systems by a Licensed Master Plumber. As licensed master plumbers, we perform LL152 inspections, certifications, and any repairs needed to keep your building compliant.

Not sure of your Community District? Look it up here, then call us to schedule your inspection.
